About Me

I am an M.Tech student in Computational and Data Science at NITK Surathkal, with a strong academic and practical foundation in Machine Learning, Data Science, and Computer Systems. My interests lie in applied machine learning, data-driven problem solving, and NLP, with a focus on building solutions that are both technically sound and practically impactful. I am driven by curiosity, enjoy learning complex systems deeply, and aspire to contribute to impactful research and industry-grade ML systems.
